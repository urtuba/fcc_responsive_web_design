Phineas Taylor Barnum

Phineas Taylor Barnum(1810, 1891) was an American businessman, showman, and entertainer. He lived in New York City, he founded two museums and a circus. He was good at being center of attention. He is known as first show business millionaire. He said "I am a showman by profession", but he was interested in writing, publishing and politics for some time.
